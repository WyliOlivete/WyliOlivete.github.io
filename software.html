Takipsilim

Survive in a land of ancient mythology...

Download from Google Play

Takipsilim is a turn-based puzzle game inspired by ancient Philippine lore. Survive on a small island by gathering resources, crafting tools, interacting with townsfolk, discovering legendary items, and receiving blessings from primordial deities to help you fight against the myriad of monsters that haunt the night. Delve into this fascinating world and discover more about Philippine mythology.

Move by swiping. Gather wood or stone, iron, gold, and diamond by tapping anywhere while in a forest or on a mountain. Interact with others by swiping into them - townsfolk can be talked to, and monsters can be attacked. Craft items in the village blacksmith and store items in the village storage. Unlock new items after every game.

Suraisu Ninja: Sukūtā Simulator

Who knew ninjas and scooters would go well together!?

Download for PC here

A fast-paced arcade game made in 48 hours for the 2016 GameJolt Bad Boxart Challenge game jam.

Ride your scooter and fly across the screen as you slice through your enemies. Get higher points by setting up combos and top the online scoreboards (if you're logged into GameJolt). With tasty pixel art, pumpy music, and a simple yet fun gameplay mechanic, you're guaranteed to have a fun time.
